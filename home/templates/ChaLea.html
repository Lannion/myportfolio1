Challenges I’ve Faced

Being a 3rd year Computer Science student has been tough especially when financial struggles become part of the journey. But even with these difficulties, I’ve stayed committed to my dream of becoming a back-end developer. Every hurdle just fuels my drive to push forward.

Acquired Life Learnings

These challenges taught me discipline, gratitude, and mental strength. I’ve learned that no matter how limited your tools may seem, growth always comes from effort. Each bug I fix, each late night I spend studying, they all build me into someone capable and persistent. It’s not just about learning code, it’s about learning life and I’m proud of how far I’ve come.
